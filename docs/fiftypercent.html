Warren Buffett once said ...

"If I was running $1 million today, or $10 million for that matter, I'd be fully invested. Anyone who says that size does not hurt investment performance is selling. The highest rates of return I've ever achieved were in the 1950s. I killed the Dow. You ought to see the numbers. But I was investing peanuts then. It's a huge structural advantage not to have a lot of money. I think I could make you 50% a year on $1 million. No, I know I could. I guarantee that."

... and ever since he was asked the same question, HOW?

1999 Berkshire Hathaway annual meeting

2007 Berkshire Hathaway annual meeting

2019 Berkshire Hathaway annual meeting

2024 Berkshire Hathaway annual meeting

Warren's answer to this question has always been surprisingly simple:

Begin by looking at companies whose names start with “A” and continue down the list.
